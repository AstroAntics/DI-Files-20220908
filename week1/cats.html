About Cats

In contrast to lions or other big cats, domestic cats are more frequently called house cats as there is (generally) no reason to distinguish them from other cats.
Cats are often prized by humans for their intellect and companionship, and worldwide there are more than seventy cat breeds!

Cat senses

  • Cats can hear sounds too faint or too high in frequency for human ears.
  • Cats can see in near-total or total darkness.
  • Cats have worse color vision - but better smell - than humans do.

Lions

Present in Africa and India, the lion typically inhabits grasslands and savannahs, but is absent in dense forests. It is usually more diurnal than other big cats, but when persecuted adapts to being active at night and at twilight. A lion pride consists of a few adult males, related females and cubs. Prides vary in size and composition from three to 20 adult lions, depending on habitat and prey availability. Females cooperate when hunting and prey mostly on large ungulates, including antelope, deer, buffalo, zebra and even giraffes.
